PERSON OF THE WEEK

Morten Rand-Hendriksen (@mor10) is a front-end developer, interaction designer, educator, speaker, and author. Originally from Norway, he now lives in Vancouver where he creates online and in-person courses as a senior staff instructor for LinkedIn Learning. Combining a masters’ degree in philosophy with his experiences working in and then teaching web development and design, Morten is passionate about applying practical ethics to design and technology and working towards a tech industry centered around ethics.
